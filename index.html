Scoring model

Strict by design, readable by everyone

Actor clarity 15 pts

Does the story identify who needs the behavior?

Preconditions 10 pts

Does it say what must be true before testing begins?

Acceptance criteria 25 pts

Are outcomes explicit, observable, and testable?

Expected outcomes 20 pts

Can a tester decide pass or fail without guessing?

Negative paths 15 pts

Are failure, invalid, expired, or unauthorized paths covered?

Vague language 15 pts

Does it avoid words like fast, easy, properly, and user-friendly?
